The Biography

A Story Written in Courage.

Growing up in Somavaram Peta, Kamareddy, Vennela faced a pivotal moment after her 10th standard. Without her father’s guidance for education, she chose to carve a path inspired by the sheer grit of athletes and the power of cinema.

Inspired by the rock-climbing sequences of Bahubali and sharing a birth date with actor Prabhas, she embraced the call of the mountains. Today, she is a certified A-Grade mountaineer representing the tribal spirit of Telangana, proving that identity is forged in action, not just heritage.

Her mission transcends physical climbing; it is a quest to revitalize the earth’s soil and protect its people through helmet safety advocacy.

Digital Impact

Collaborating with top-tier Content Creators on Instagram, Vennela has built a digital community to amplify her core missions:

  • 1
    Helmet Safety: Leveraging influencer networks to normalize protective gear in rural and urban youth.
  • 2
    Women Empowerment: Collaborative storytelling on Instagram to show girls that financial barriers are just hills, not mountains.
